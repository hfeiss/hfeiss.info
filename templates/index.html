Hello World!

The Art, Science, and Business of Brewing Beer: I thought the college class would quench a simple curiosity. To the surprise of many, it started a career. For years to follow, I spent more time studying the microbiology of fermentation than quantum mechanics, and ended up presenting the fluid dynamics of brewhouse whirlpools to fellow physicists more than once.

Through the years at Breakside Brewery, I worked up the ranks and helped our team grow from 7 to 30. My last position there was a dream: running an award winning Research and Development brewery in Portland, Oregon with both autonomy and assistants. I loved the camaraderie, fast-paced teamwork, and creativity among the all the brewers. However, the problems I was solving, I didn't personally meaningful.

After 4 years of growth and streamlining, I was ready for a new challenge. I realized that I would rather find insights in piles of messy data than pint glasses. To satisfy my thirst for knowledge, I decided to switch from one of the oldest professions to the one of the newest: Data Science.
